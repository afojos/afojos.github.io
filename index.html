About

The analyst behind
the numbers.

I started where most people are afraid to look — tracking every single Naira I earned and spent for 4 months. The result was uncomfortable: a 52/100 health score, a ₦80,000 data bundle bill, and a savings rate of 4.6% against a 20% target.

That audit became Naira Moves — a financial discipline system built on a simple belief: the numbers don't lie, but you have to be willing to look at them.

I'm a self-taught financial data analyst building a portfolio of 8 real projects using real Nigerian financial data — annual reports, CBN datasets, Kaggle transaction records, and my own finances. No textbook simulations.

I'm currently completing the Microsoft Power BI certification and the Google Data Analytics Certificate while building this portfolio publicly on LinkedIn and Instagram.

Available for: personal finance audits, financial modelling, Power BI dashboard builds, SME analysis, and remote data projects.
