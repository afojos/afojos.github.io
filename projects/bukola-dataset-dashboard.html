Red Flags Identified
Issues found applying the Naira Moves framework
🍽️
Dining Out = ₦812,400 (20.6% of all spend)
The single largest expense category — more than housing, utilities, or transport. Reducing to ₦500K would free up ₦312K to push savings rate above 25%.
📅
September Spike — Consistently +34% above average
Every September, spend jumps dramatically — especially dining on Sundays and Mondays. This seasonal pattern is predictable and therefore preventable with a monthly cap.
⚖️
80.6% Expense Ratio — Above the healthy threshold
A healthy expense ratio is 60–70%. At 80.6%, this person is one unexpected bill away from dipping into savings. Emergency buffer is thin.
🔄
No Pattern Control on Home Improvement
Home improvement purchases always land on Thursdays and Fridays — a recurring, unbudgeted pattern. These should be planned and capped monthly rather than reactive.
